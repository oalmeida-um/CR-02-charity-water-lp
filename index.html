Our Story

Founded in 2006, charity: water has funded over 64,000 water projects in 29 countries, providing clean water to over 15 million people. We believe that clean water is a basic human right and are committed to transparency and accountability in all our projects.

Real Impact: Meet Amina

Amina used to walk miles every day to collect water for her family. Thanks to your support, her village now has a clean water well. Amina can go to school and her family is healthier and happier.
